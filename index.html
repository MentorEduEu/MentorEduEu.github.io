What we do?

Mentor Education is an LCC company based in Belgrade, Serbia. It promotes, organises and manages educational programs and internships for students, teaching staff, young workers and organisations from all over Europe.

In Mentor Education we aim to bring people together and provide high-quality educational and vocational opportunities. focusing on environmental responsibility and sustainable practices.

Mentor Education is founded by two young women with an academic background in education, communication and culture, who want to share their vision about a better world by promoting education and culture.

We believe in teamwork, creating a network with the best professionals, tutors and volunteers and we intend to encourage participation in European programs, supporting the development of the European Education Area.
